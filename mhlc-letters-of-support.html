On the record.

Federal, state, academic, medical, and community leaders have put their names behind the Memory Health Life Center. The letters below are excerpted with attribution; full signed letters are on file with the Wood County Health Care Foundation.

Milestone · May 2023

$6 million federal earmark secured.

The U.S. House Agriculture Subcommittee released its earmarks and the foundation's full request was funded for the clinic and education wing of the Memory Health Life Center — the first major piece of the campaign's funding stack.

"Congressman Gooden is excited to see this through."

— Claire Alden, Legislative Director, Office of Rep. Lance Gooden (TX-05)
