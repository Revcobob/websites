Stewardship

Every contribution matters.

Each and every contribution is important as the Wood County Health Care Foundation, a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit, creates this one-of-a-kind education, research, and living laboratory facility adjacent to the Quitman hospital.

Total projected costs are in excess of $60,000,000 and every philanthropic, tax-deductible contribution brings us closer to our goal. Major contributors will have options for naming recognition, so please give prayerful consideration with your support. It is a great way to honor a loved one who has dementia or Alzheimer's.
