For Foundation Members

Have an event to post?

Foundation board members, committee chairs, and approved community partners can submit events for the calendar. Send the date, location, description, and any RSVP details to the foundation and we'll publish it here.

Submit an event (903) 760-9224

What to include

  • Title and date · the headline and the calendar slot
  • Time and venue · including address and parking notes
  • Short description · two to three sentences about why people should come
  • Category · caregiver, community, foundation, or fundraiser
  • RSVP details · contact name, phone, email, or registration link
