Donor FAQ

Questions donors ask.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. The Wood County Health Care Foundation is a 501(c)(3) public charity, EIN 75-2309177. Your gift is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt by email after your donation completes.

Do I need a PayPal account?

No. PayPal handles secure payment processing, but you can pay with any major credit or debit card without creating a PayPal account.

Can I give in honor or memory of someone?

Yes — and we encourage it. Use the "Dedicate this gift" option on the form. Honorees can be added to our donor honor roll on request, and we'll notify the family of the honoree if you provide their contact information.

What about naming opportunities?

Major contributors have options for naming recognition — rooms, gardens, programs, and more. Please contact us directly to discuss a major or naming gift; our team will walk you through the options.

How is my gift used?

100% of donations are stewarded for one purpose: building and sustaining the Memory Health Life Center on the 29-acre site adjacent to UT Health Quitman.

Can I give appreciated stock or from an IRA?

Yes — both can offer significant tax advantages. Contact the foundation directly and we'll send transfer instructions and our broker information, or the language your IRA custodian needs for a qualified charitable distribution (QCD).
