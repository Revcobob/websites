Parliamentary & Procedural Consulting.

Through Robert Haney's national role as a parliamentary authority and past President of the American Society of Legislative Clerks and Secretaries, the firm consults to legislative bodies on the rules, technology, and procedures that make modern chambers work.

In practice

Legislative modernization

Advising chambers on the transition from paper-era procedure to integrated digital workflows. Robert led the Texas House through the electronic-filing transition that retired a century-old paper process and now consults nationally on the same work.

Rules and procedure review

Working with leadership and clerks' offices on standing rules, calendar mechanics, and floor management. Procedure is the operating system of a chamber; ours is the kind of practice that updates it without crashing it.

Chamber technology

Specifying, evaluating, and overseeing the systems that modern legislative bodies depend on — from bill-management platforms to vote recording to journal production. Independent counsel, not vendor advocacy.

Private-client procedural counsel

The same procedural fluency benefits private clients in Texas engagements where the rule matters as much as the relationship. Calendar postures, point-of-order risk, and amendment paths are not afterthoughts — they decide outcomes.

Who hires us for this

  • State legislatures and their clerks' offices.
  • Parliamentary and procedural bodies seeking independent counsel.
  • Private clients with Texas matters where procedure is decisive.
