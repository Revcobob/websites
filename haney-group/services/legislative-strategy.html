Legislative Strategy & Advocacy.

We represent clients through every stage of the Texas legislative process — from pre-filing through floor consideration, conference, and the governor's desk. The work is built around relationships that took decades to earn and procedural fluency that cannot be improvised.

In practice

Bill strategy

Who carries it, who chairs it, who refers it, where it sits on the calendar — answered before the bill is dropped, not after. We work backward from the realistic vote count and forward from the substantive policy. Both directions matter.

Committee work

Witness preparation, member outreach, committee-substitute drafting, and amendment tracking — on a daily basis during session. We sit through the hearings nobody covers and know what was actually said by whom.

Coalition building

Aligning industry, association, and statewide allies into a single message — and, where it matters, into a single hearing room. We do not assemble coalitions for press releases; we assemble them for vote counts.

Testimony preparation

Drafting, rehearsing, and post-hearing follow-up for client principals and external witnesses. We prepare for the question every member is actually going to ask, not the question the talking points wish they were going to ask.

Interim engagement

Charge tracking, agency outreach, and the quiet conversations that decide what next session looks like. The most important work of a session is almost always done between sessions.

Who hires us for this

  • Statewide trade associations whose priorities can be moved or defended.
  • Regulated companies with a clear Texas policy interest.
  • Health care organizations navigating both bill and budget.
  • Local governments and special districts facing preemption or seeking statutory fixes.
  • Coalitions in motion that need a disciplined center of gravity.
