Selected Engagements

A representative sample of the work clients hire us for.

Engagements are presented anonymously where client confidentiality requires it. Each follows the same structure — situation, role, outcome — because that is the only structure that matters in a lobby-firm review.

Statewide Association · Legislative Strategy

The Situation

A statewide trade association faced legislation that threatened the licensure framework its members had operated under for a generation. The bill carried a plausible coalition and a sympathetic press frame.

Our Role

We rewrote the association's positioning, prepared member testimony for two committees, walked the bill's language through three rounds of amendment, and coordinated a coalition of unlikely allies into a single hearing.

The Outcome

The licensure framework held. The board exited the session with the language it asked for.

Health Care · Appropriations

The Situation

A health care client needed a specific Medicaid policy direction reflected in the appropriations act — the kind of priority that lives in a rider and dies in conference if it isn't watched.

Our Role

We engaged the relevant Article II subcommittee, the LBB staff, and the chair's office on a parallel track, and prepared the client's leadership for two hearings and one closed-door briefing.

The Outcome

The rider survived conference and shaped the following biennium's agency direction.

Local Government · Preemption Defense

The Situation

A Texas city faced statewide legislation that would have stripped a long-standing local authority. The city had no in-session lobbying presence and limited time to mount a defense.

Our Role

We built a coalition of similarly affected cities, prepared mayoral testimony, briefed the chair's office on the practical operating impact, and authored amendment language that preserved local authority while accommodating the bill's stated purpose.

The Outcome

The amendment was adopted. The city retained the authority. The coalition has continued into interim.

Infrastructure · Capital Appropriations

The Situation

An infrastructure operator needed a multi-biennium capital appropriation to advance a long-deferred project that crossed three regulatory jurisdictions.

Our Role

We sequenced the appropriations request alongside the agency's exceptional item, prepared regional coalition testimony, and managed the press posture across two markets so that statewide officials saw a unified, non-controversial case.

The Outcome

The capital appropriation was funded across the biennium with bipartisan support.

Coalition · Communications & Crisis

The Situation

A multi-employer coalition faced a sudden, high-profile press cycle that threatened a year of careful legislative groundwork.

Our Role

We rebuilt the coalition's message architecture in seventy-two hours, briefed every coalition principal on the new through-line, and managed the on-the-record posture across four reporters and two committee chairs.

The Outcome

The cycle resolved without legislative consequence. The coalition emerged with stronger discipline than it had before the cycle began.

Institutional · Parliamentary Consulting

The Situation

A state legislative chamber sought to modernize its journaling and bill-management systems without disrupting an active session.

Our Role

We provided independent counsel on requirements, evaluated vendors against the chamber's actual operating rhythms, and authored the rules and training materials that accompanied the transition.

The Outcome

The transition was completed between sessions and ran cleanly through the next gavel-in.
