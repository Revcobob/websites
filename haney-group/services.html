How an engagement works

The first conversation. Short, confidential, no obligation. We ask what you are trying to accomplish, when it has to be accomplished by, and what has already been tried. We tell you whether we are the right firm — and if so, how we would approach the work.

The plan. A written engagement plan that names committees, members, agencies, milestones, and the realistic best- and worst-case outcomes. No vague references to "strategic engagement."

The work. Principal-led, every meeting. We staff the matter with the discipline the matter requires; we do not pad the team.

The after. A written debrief — what moved, what did not, what the interim looks like, and what next session needs to look like to finish the job.
